Yadong Li

First-year ECE Ph.D. Student@University of Washington

I am an incoming Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering at the University of Washington. I am a group member of the Networking and Emerging Wireless Technologies (NEWT) Lab, advised by Prof. Akshay Gadre.

Previously, I was a group member of the USTC IP Lab, advised by Prof. Qibin Sun and Prof. Yan Chen, where I focused on developing human-centric sensing systems with wireless devices.
